Chilli Tofu Recipe

Ingredients

  • 800g firm tofu
  • Cornflour
  • 2tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 brown onion
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 3cm piece ginger

Instructions

  1. Spread the tofu on a large plate and dust with the cornflour, turning the cubes to coat.
  2. Take a deep frying pan for which you have a lid and add enough oil to come 0.5cm up the sides, and heat over a medium flame.
  3. Line a dish with kitchen towel, to drain the cooked tofu on.
  4. Shake any excess cornflour off the tofu, then put half the tofu in the hot oil.
  5. Fry for three minutes, turning regularly with tongs, until golden.
  6. Then transfer to the paper-lined dish and repeat with the remaining tofu.
